### Quick posting format (suggested)
- **Claim:** what you’re saying  
- **Context:** optional  
- **Prompt:** what you want others to argue with  

### Rules (short)
Debate is welcome. Harassment isn’t.  
No threats, doxxing, or calls for real-world violence.  
Credit artists. Label graphic content.
